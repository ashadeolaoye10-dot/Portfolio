Prayers Read

☀ Morning Prayer

Begin your day with God's guidance and strength.

Heavenly Father, thank You for the gift of today. Guide my thoughts, words and actions. Give me wisdom, strength and peace. Help me honor You in all I do. In Jesus' name, Amen.

🌙 Evening Prayer

End your day with gratitude and reflection.

Lord, thank You for carrying me through today. Forgive my mistakes and renew my heart. Grant me peaceful rest tonight. In Jesus' name, Amen.

🙌 Thanksgiving Prayer

Give thanks for God's goodness and mercy.

Father, I thank You for Your provision, protection and faithfulness. May my heart remain grateful in every season. Amen.

❤️ Healing Prayer

Seek God's healing and restoration.

Lord, touch every area of my life that needs healing. Bring restoration, peace and strength. Let Your healing power flow through me. Amen.

🛡 Protection Prayer

Ask God for protection over your life.

Heavenly Father, surround me with Your protection. Keep me safe from harm and guide my steps. Let Your presence go before me. Amen.

💡 Guidance Prayer

Ask God for wisdom and direction.

Lord, direct my paths and help me make wise decisions. Reveal Your will and purpose for my life. Lead me in righteousness. Amen.
